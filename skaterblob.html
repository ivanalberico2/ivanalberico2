Skater Blob on Unity 3D (Virtual Reality I)


Ivan Alberico     Adrian Hartmann    

Institute of Machine Tools and Manufacturing, ETH Zurich


Game Media

The game showcases a skateboarding character with a unique blob shape. It offers three intricately designed levels, each presenting a variety of challenges and quests. Players can engage in tasks such as collecting hidden coins within a time limit, skillfully sliding on rails and ramps without falling, solving riddles, and more.
